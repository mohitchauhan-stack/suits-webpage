About

Suits is an American legal drama series that follows a talented college dropout, Mike Ross , who is hired by a top New York City law firm without a law degree. The show centers on his partnership with the senior partner, Harvey Specter , to win cases while keeping Mike's secret from the rest of the firm. It ran for nine seasons from 2011 to 2019 and stars Gabriel Macht as Harvey Specter and Patrick J. Adams as Mike Ross.
